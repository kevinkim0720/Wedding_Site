About Us

Kevin and Amy were both members and served for Agape Christian Fellowship (ACF) at JMU. One day, Kevin decided to courageously ask Amy. He was rejected at first, but Amy gave him a chance to get to know him better.

After months of talking and hanging out, she decided to give him a chance thus their relationship started in March 2019. As they continued to date and do life together, their bond grew more and more. They shared amazing adventures, lots of laughter and love, and unforgetable moments.

After 5 years of dating, Kevin asked THE question, and Amy said YES! Blessed and filled with joy, they can't wait to start their next chapter as Husband and Wife
