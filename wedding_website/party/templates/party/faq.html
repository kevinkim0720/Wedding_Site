FAQ

What is the dress code? +
The dress code is formal attire. Please no jeans or denim. Ladies, please refrain from wearing white and champagne-colored dresses.
Will it be indoors or outdoors? +
Ceremony and Reception are held indoors. Some guests may be outdoors during Cocktail Hour due to spacing.
Can I bring a plus-one? +
Due to limited space and budget, we are unable to accommodate additional guests. Please RSVP only for the names listed on the invitation. Thank you!
Can I bring children? +
Although we would love to have your children celebrate with us, please kindly refrain from bringing them unless you are family.
Is there free parking? +
There is a main parking area when you enter the venue (on the right-hand side). Handicapped spaces are available right outside the venue.
Is it an open bar? +
YES! Please enjoy as much as you want. We want everyone to have a good time.
Is there a Wedding Registry? +
If you would like to give, we kindly ask that you consider making a monetary contribution in lieu of traditional wedding gifts.
If you have any other questions, contact Kevin or Amy.
